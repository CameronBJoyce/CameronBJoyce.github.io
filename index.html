Data Science

On top of my academic and professional work, I also do freelance analytics for companies and individuals. This can be as simple as getting you important summary statistics about your market demographic, your company, and your consumer base. I also can delve deep into your company's data and locate the key value drivers and the routes to optimize your business strategy. My approach is holistic, not just looking at financial indicators, but examing everything from GIS data (your physical location) to the individual consumers. Decisions are made from data. Let me make accessing and analyzing that data easy for you so you can make the best decisions. Email me at bennett1423@gmail.com if you are interested in working together or learning more about what I can do for you or your company.
